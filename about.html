About The Little Remnant

"For the preaching of the cross is to them that perish foolishness; but unto us which are saved it is the power of God." - 1 Corinthians 1:18

Our Heritage

Since 1880, The Little Remnant has stood as a beacon of truth in Western Australia. We are a Sabbath-keeping congregation dedicated to preserving and sharing the original teachings of the Seventh Day Adventist faith while maintaining our independent status.

Our Mission

We are committed to spreading the special 'testing truths' for our time through both traditional and modern means. As guided by the Spirit, we proclaim these truths not in a tame, spiritless way, but with the demonstration of God's power and authority.

Our Identity

We believe, as stated in the Upward Look: "God has a church. It is not the great cathedral, nor the national establishment, but it is the people who love God and keep His commandments." We strive to be that church, a community of believers who are committed to living out our faith in every aspect of our lives.

Get Involved

We invite you to join us in our mission. Whether through attending our services, participating in community outreach, or engaging in Bible study, there are many ways to get involved and grow in your faith with us.
